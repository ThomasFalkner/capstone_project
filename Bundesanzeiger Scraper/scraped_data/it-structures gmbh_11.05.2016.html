Close Brothers Asset Finance GmbH

Frankfurt am Main

Jahresabschluss zum Geschäftsjahr vom 01.01.2015 bis zum 31.07.2015

CLOSE BROTHERS LIMITED

Company number: 195626

(the "Company")

Minutes of a meeting of the Board of Directors of the Company held at

10 Crown Place, London, EC2A 4FT on Thursday 28 January 2016 at 1.55 PM.

Present: Stephen Hodges (Chairman)
  Malcolm Hook  
  Adrian Sainsbury  
In attendance: John Hudspith (Secretary)

1. CHAIRMAN

Stephen Hodges was appointed Chairman of the meeting.

2. NOTICE AND QUORUM

The Chairman advised that due notice of the meeting had been given to all directors of the Company and that a quorum of the Board was present. Accordingly, the Chairman declared the meeting open.

3. BUSINESS OF THE MEETING

The Chairman reported that the purposes of the meeting were to consider: (i) amending the objects of the Company's subsidiary Close Brothers Asset Finance GmbH as well as a change of its directors; (ii) the grant of a power of attorney to represent the Company in order to bring these into effect; and (iii) adopting the Close Brothers Asset Finance GmbH balance sheet as at 31 July 2015.

4. DECLARATIONS OF INTEREST

4.1 The Chairman noted that any situations where the Directors' interests may conflict with those of the Company had been duly authorised. Each of the Directors at the meeting declared the nature and extent of their interests (or noted previous declarations of interest), if any, directly or indirectly, in the matters to be discussed as required by Sections 177 and 182 of the Companies Act 2006 (as amended) (the "Act")and in accordance with the Company's Articles of Association.

4.2 It was noted that, pursuant to Article 16 of the Company's Articles of Association, subject where applicable to disclosure and subject to any terms imposed by the Directors, a Director may vote and form part of the quorum in relation to any matter in which he is directly or indirectly interested. No Director at the meeting was for any reason disqualified from voting at the meeting or forming part of the said quorum.

5. AMENDMENT OF OBJECTS AND CHANGE OF DIRECTORS, GERMANY

The Chairman advised the meeting that Close Brothers Asset Finance GmbH ('CBAF') is submitting an application to BaFin (Federal Financial Supervisory Authority) for a licence to conduct a leasing business in Germany. Pending receipt of a banking licence, the Company is looking to conduct cross border transactions in Germany on a passport basis. In order to permit CBAF to commence operations contacting potential clients on behalf of the Company , it is necessary to amend the objects of CBAF. lt was proposed that the object clause 3.1 of the Articles of Association be amended by deleting the text:

"The purpose and object of the company shall be the management of the company's assets";

and replacing it with the text:

'The purpose and object of the company shall be the management of the company's assets and to support the acquisition of new customers for Close Brothers Limited for financing solutions for business clients'.

The Chairman explained that CBAF has received advice from Freshfields Germany to amend the governance model for CBAF so that it has a structure which will satisfy BaFin requirements as well as oversight from the Company as sole shareholder, it is proposed to appoint Bruno Oestreicher as a managing director and to accept the resignations of Michael Randall and Rachelle Frewer as managing directors of that company,

There was produced to the meeting an `Application to the Commercial Register' providing for the amendment to the company's objects and the changes of directors. The Directors gave due consideration to the proposals and concluded that it is in the best interests of the Company's business and to the commercial benefit and advantage of the Company, to implement these alterations.

IT WAS RESOLVED THAT:

(i)

the amendment of the Glase Brothers Asset Finance GmbH objects as detailed above be approved;

(ii)

the appointment of Bruno Oestreicher as a managing director of Close Brothers Asset Finance GmbH be approved;

(iii)

the resignations of Michael Randall and Rachelle Frewer as managing directors of Close Brothers Asset Finance GmbH be approved; and

(iv)

the 'Application to the Commercial Register' providing for the amendment to the company's objects and the changes of directors, be approved,

6. POWER OF ATTORNEY, GERMANY

There was produced to the meeting a power of attorney authorising Michael Vander to act on behalf of the Company at a shareholder's meeting of CBAF in order to effect the amendment of that company's objects and to make changes to its managing directors (the 'Power of Attorney').

After careful consideration of the duties of the directors under the Companies Act 2006 and all factors relevant to those duties (including the factors at Section 172 (1) of the Companies Act 2006 that were relevant to such consideration) the Directors gave due consideration to the proposal and concluded that it is in the best interests of the Company's business and to the commercial benefit and advantage of the Company, to provide the power of attorney.

IT WAS RESOLVED THAT:

(i)

the Power of Attorney be approved;
(ii)

the appointment of Michael Vander to act on behalf of the Company at a shareholder's meeting of Close Brothers Asset Finance GmbH in order to effect the amendment of that company's objects and to make changes to its managing directors be approved; and

(iii)

the authority to be granted to Michael Vander acting individually, be approved.

7. CLOSE BROTHERS ASSET FINANCE GMBH BALANCE SHEET

There was produced to the meeting the unaudited CBAF balance sheet at 31 July 2015 signed by the three managing directors. lt was explained that under German law the shareholder is required to approve the balance sheet and to discharge the Geschäftsführung.

The meeting noted that as the company has not yet traded it is not required to produce audited financial statements and a management report and accordingly the accounts only show cash at bank and shareholder's equity. The Balance Sheet has been agreed to the Company's SAP records.

IT WAS RESOLVED THAT the Close Brothers Asset Finance GmbH balance sheet at 31 July 2015 be adopted and that the Geschäftsführung be discharged.

8. CLOSE

There being no further business, the Chairman declared the meeting closed.

 

CHAIRMAN
